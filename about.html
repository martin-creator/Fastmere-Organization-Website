We live for green work for green

We are committed to the supply and production of quality fresh produce in Uganda and are leveraging the use of technology and innovations to increase productivity and profitability for all stakeholders in the value chain. We understand that building a reliable distribution channel is key in improving market access for farmers and hence we make it our mission to devise ways we can improve the current food distribution channel in Uganda.

MISSION

Be the leading suppliers of quality fresh produce from smallholder farmers and increase their productivity and profitability through extension service programs.

VISION

Build sustainable food secure communities.

Meet the founding team

Maria Ndagire

Founder & Team Lead

Ndagire Moreen Maria is an Agricultural engineer with a degree from Earth University of Costa Rica. As the current CEO and founder of Fastmere, she is passionate about designing sustainable bussiness models for rural farming communities as a way to alleviate poverty. Maria has worked with starthub Africa an organization that shapes the next generation of entrepreneurs through training and mentorship.
Maria has also worked with food for the hungry and co-authored a manual that guides the youth through entrepreneurship to start businesses in rural areas and worked with restless development prior to that as Youth think tank researcher on the topic of young people’s adoption and adaption to technology in Agriculture where she had a chance to interview , assess and validate problems surrounding young peoples involvement in Agriculture.
